Congrats to Virgin America on the launch of their new responsive website - only the 2nd major airline to launch a fully responsive site. The first airline to launch a responsive site was Swiss, only a few weeks back. Virgin America has also launched a nifty "how it works" site to explain their thinking behind the redesign and the core principles that they believe create a better Customer experience. According to Virgin America the goal of the redesign was to make for a faster, smoother and more intuitive booking experience - whether guests are on a laptop, tablet or smartphone.
We wanted to create a fresh, new online face for Virgin America that better reflects the design-driven, tech-forward experience guests find onboard our planes. The responsive design-based site will offer a faster, smoother and more intuitive booking experience – whether guests are visiting virginamerica.com from a laptop, a tablet or a smartphone.
So far the reviews from Customers and industry experts are mixed:
